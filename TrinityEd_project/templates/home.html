Our Mission

TrinityEd is a simple, affordable tool that brings together student attendance, grades, and fee information to spot students who might be at risk of dropping out. We help teachers and mentors act early to support these students, turning data into helpful alerts that make a real difference in keeping kids in school.

"From scattered spreadsheets to actionable alerts: supporting students, mentors, and parents through early warning signals."

How It Works

TrinityEd makes it easy to keep track of students and help them succeed:

1. Gather Information

It pulls together data on attendance, grades, and fees from your existing records with simple uploads or connections.

2. Spot Issues Early

Smart analysis identifies patterns that might signal a student is struggling before it becomes a bigger problem.

3. Send Alerts

Notifications go out to mentors and parents through email or WhatsApp so they can step in quickly when needed.

4. Easy Dashboard

View clear charts and insights on a user-friendly screen that's simple to navigate and understand.

For Mentors

TrinityEd helps mentors support students better with tools designed for real classroom needs:

Early Alerts

Get notifications about students who need help based on their attendance, grades, and fees, so you can act before it's too late.

Clear Insights

Understand why a student might be at risk with straightforward explanations that build trust in the system.

Easy to Learn

The simple design means you can start using it right away without much training or technical knowledge.

Focused Notifications

Only get alerts for the most important issues to avoid overload and keep your focus on what matters.
